# Credit Card Customer Clustering

## Overview
This project focuses on customer segmentation using clustering techniques on credit card customer data. The objective is to group customers based on their spending behavior, credit usage, and transaction patterns to identify meaningful customer segments.

Customer clustering helps financial institutions understand customer behavior and enables targeted marketing, personalized services, and better business decision-making.

---

## Problem Statement
Credit card companies deal with customers having different spending habits and financial behaviors. Identifying groups of similar customers can help in:
- Customer segmentation
- Personalized offers
- Risk assessment
- Marketing strategies
- Customer retention

This project uses unsupervised machine learning techniques to cluster customers based on their financial attributes.

---

## Dataset
The dataset contains information related to credit card customers and their transaction behavior.

### Features may include:
